About the Project

Why we built this?

We created this website as part of our web-tech project. As first-year BCA students at SVGU, we wanted to build a simple, digital way for students to learn about academic ethics through interactive quizzes.

Our Motto

"Learning with Integrity."

Our goal is to make sure every student understands the importance of honest work while having a smooth, user-friendly experience.

What's Next?

This started as a group project, but we aren't stopping here! We want to enroll this platform into our college's ERP system. Our vision is to make this a standard tool for the university, where:

  • New students can easily complete their Academic requirements.
  • Results are automatically tracked for the college records.
  • The quiz becomes a permanent part of our campus digital life.
